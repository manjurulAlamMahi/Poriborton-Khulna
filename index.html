ABOUT Paribartan-Khulna

“PARIBARTAN-Khulna” is a non-government social development organization based in the southwest coastal region of Bangladesh, working for ensuring socio-economic development with a prime focus on environmental conservation & rights through financial an cultural development of the underprivileged coastal communitiess

Our mission & vision

These define us as an organization and guide all of our actions :

  • VISION :

    Ensuring livelihood security and rights of the resource poor coastal community of the South-west Coastal Region.

  • MISSION :

    Strengthening vulnerable under privileged coastal community with the tools needed to combat the impediments for securing livelihood and rights.
